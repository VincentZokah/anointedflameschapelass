Our history

Anointed Flames Chapel which was formed by Yahuah through His servant Rev.Mrs.Alice Adom Nyarkoah Aboagye Yeboah the leader of the church. She had her calling in the early year 1990 where she begun her evangelism and prophetic ministry.After a while, she travelled overseas to start a new life because of how tedious and persecuting the work of God was.After three years of leaving overseas, the Lord brought her back to her motherland.She returned back to do the work of God again and there aftert he church and registered on 1st March 2011.The church has grown to have about 5(five) branches in some of the regions in Ghana and more are yet to be establised.
